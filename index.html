About

A career built on
community and craft

David O'Dell is among the most seasoned arts executives in the American nonprofit sector — a leader whose career spans opera companies, symphonies, arts councils, and community organizations from the Research Triangle to the Rocky Mountains.

His work is grounded in a conviction that the arts are not a luxury but a civic necessity — that communities with robust arts participation are more connected, more resilient, and more fully themselves.

Cairn Arts Advisors draws on four decades of executive leadership, fundraising, board development, and community engagement to help organizations find their direction and lead with purpose.

In every organization he has led, contributed income has grown. That record reflects disciplined strategy, sustained donor relationships, and the conviction that arts organizations can and should ask boldly.

"The arts don't decorate civic life — they define it."

Services

Where we can
help you most

01

Strategic Planning & Organizational Assessment

Honest evaluation of where your organization stands — and a clear, actionable path forward. Built on deep sector knowledge and decades of executive experience.

02

Fundraising & Development Infrastructure

From donor cultivation strategy to CRM implementation, we help organizations build the systems and relationships that sustain long-term community support.

03

Board Development & Governance

Boards that govern well are the foundation of thriving arts organizations. We help boards understand their role, build their capacity, and lead with confidence.

04

Interim Executive Leadership

Experienced, steady leadership during transitions. Cairn Arts Advisors can step in as interim executive director — maintaining momentum while your organization finds its next permanent leader.

05

Community Engagement Strategy

Access and welcome for everyone — not as a slogan but as an operational reality. We help organizations build genuine, lasting connections across the full breadth of their communities.

06

Campaign Counsel & Major Gifts

Capital campaigns, artist residency programs, endowment initiatives. Senior counsel for high-stakes fundraising moments that require experience, judgment, and relationships.
